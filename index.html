About the Island

"Helen of the West Indies"

St. Lucia is a beautiful Caribean island bordering the Atlantic Ocean. It is a perfect destination for getaways offering natual landscapes of miles long beaches, lushous forests, natural waterfalls and volcanic mountains.

The Perfect Destination

Tourism is a vital part of the island's economy and is expected to grow. Many like to come here for tropical weather, green forests, resorts and beaches.

Things to Do

The most known island's attraction is the twin mountains "The Pitons". Some of the other destinatins are the volcanos, Sulphur Springs, rain forests, the Botanical Gardens, and numerous parks
